The Heroku-vs-CloudFormation framing
- Many users want Kubernetes to be "a Heroku": a platform that abstracts away the OS and distributed-systems details so you just ship apps
- Kubernetes actually sees itself as closer to "CloudFormation": sufficient to define your entire infrastructure, but generic over the underlying cloud provider
